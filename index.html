About us

Small counter. Big respect for the fish.

Geo Sushi Bar started with one idea: sushi doesn't need to be complicated to be excellent. Every piece of nigiri is pressed to order, every roll is rolled when you ask for it, and every bowl of rice is seasoned the same way it was the day we opened.

We keep the menu wide enough to explore — from simple maguro to a chef's-special chirashi bowl — but never so wide that anything sits around waiting to be fresh. If it's on the counter, it was cut today.
